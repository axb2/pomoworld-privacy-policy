Privacy Policy for PomoWorld

Effective Date: February 24, 2025

1. Introduction

PomoWorld ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you use our mobile application ("App").

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

When you create an account or subscribe to our services, we may collect personal information such as your email address.

2.2 Usage Data

We automatically collect information about how you interact with the App, including device information, usage statistics, and preferences. This data is used to enhance your user experience.

2.3 Database Storage

Data saved to our database—such as session details, progress, and settings—is stored securely and is used solely for improving the functionality of the App.

3. Third-Party Services

We use third-party services, including Firebase for authentication and data storage, to provide and improve our App. These services have their own privacy policies which govern their data collection and usage practices.
